Overview

In this experiment, you will initially receive $5.00 (in addition to the show-up fee). There are 40 periods in this experiment. In each period, you will be in one of two roles: (i) the Chooser or (ii) the Offeror. The role that you are initially assigned to will be your role throughout the experiment. Half of the subjects in the session are Choosers and half are Offerors. In each period, a Chooser and an Offeror will be randomly paired.
